Quick Answers

Common questions before you inquire.

Short answers for overseas clients comparing Japanese custom homes, renovation, and Osaka/Kansai builders.

Can overseas clients consult in English or Chinese?

Yes. Use the inquiry form in English or Chinese; it prepares an email with your project type, preferred contact method, budget range, and questions.

Which areas does Takumi Komuten serve?

Takumi Komuten is based in Izumisano, Osaka and is best suited for custom home, renovation, store, and office projects in Osaka and the wider Kansai area.

Is the instant estimate a formal quotation?

No. It is a low starter range for early planning. A formal quote depends on site conditions, structure, materials, permits, and detailed scope.

What performance topics can I ask about?

You can ask about WB ventilated construction, insulation, ZEH-oriented energy planning, seismic readiness, and climate-aware renovation for Japanese homes.
