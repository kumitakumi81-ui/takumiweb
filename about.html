Company

A Kansai builder with a personal standard.

Since 1987, Takumi Komuten has worked from Izumisano with a simple idea: build healthy homes with the sincerity of making our own.

Philosophy

Forward movement and effort, translated into daily comfort.

The company’s base message is healthy housing for the people who live there. In practice, that means climate-aware construction, careful material decisions, and a team that supports new builds and renovations with local experience.

The point is not to look Japanese. The point is to live well in Japan.

For global customers, a house in Japan can be difficult to understand from afar. This site makes the decision path clearer: who the builder is, what they build, how comfort is engineered, and how to begin a conversation.
